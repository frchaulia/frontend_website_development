Profile

Polinema is a vocational higher education institution located in the city of Malang. Polinema continues to develop to become a superior vocational education institution and ready to compete in the global world. Polinema has an innovative education system and competitive skills that are globally needed by industry, government agencies and society.

Polinema supports applied research and community service in the fields of science and technology development as well as community welfare. Polinema is also committed to implementing an education management system with good governance principles. Polinema also believes that a conducive academic atmosphere is very important to improve the quality of human resources and teaching that supports lifelong learning and the growth of an entrepreneurial spirit.

Goals

  1. Encouraging Students To Be Innovative, Creative, And Competent In Their Fields
  2. Realizing Students Awareness of Rights, Obligations, and Responsibilities as Embodiments

Vision

To be a Leading Vocational Higher Education Institution in the Global Competition.

Mission

  1. To develop and provide quality, innovative, and globally competitive vocational education which encourage long life learning and entrepreneurship spirit in accordance with the needs of industry, government institutions, and society.
  2. To conduct applied research for the development of science and technology and the improvement of community welfare.
  3. To conduct community services for improving community welfare.
  4. To run education management system based on the principles of good governance.
  5. To develop mutual cooperation with various parties at national and international level in relevant areas.
