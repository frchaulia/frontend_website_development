SNMPN Path Registration

Intended for students who have achievements in academic and non-academic fields.

  • Graduated in the same year.
  • Schools are required to have NPSN.
  • Students Must Have NISN.
  • Students get recommendations from schools.

SBMPN Path Registration

The selection path for written exams is carried out simultaneously by State Polytechnics throughout Indonesia. For those who are interested, please register yourself by reading the information first on the Polinemadi web address admisi.polinema.ac.id

  • Have a NISN.
  • High school graduate, high school, MA / Pursue package C.
  • Have a diploma / SKL.
  • Haven't been accepted into the SNMPN track in that year.
  • Pay the registration fee of 200,000.

KIP-K

Tuition fee assistance to prospective students who have academic potential and come from underprivileged families. Participants who wish to register for the KIP Lecture must obtain a recommendation from the Principal to register at the address kip-gulung.kemdikbud.go.id.

  • High school students or equivalent graduates in 2020, 2021, and 2022.
  • Have a valid NISN, NPSN, and NIK.
  • Have high academic potential but come from a poor family.
  • Ullamco laboris nisi ut aliquip ex ea commodo consequatEconomically (proven by valid documents) 4 Rent SMA/SMK/MA/equivalent graduates in 2022 have good academic potential and have a KIP Card or Prosperous Family Card or are recorded in the Social Welfare Integrated Data (DTKS) of the Ministry of Social Affairs.
  • Pass the selection at PTN or PTS in study programs (A or B accreditation) and allow study programs with C accreditation but with certain considerations.

Independen Path

An alternative path for those of you who have followed the selection of the SNMPN or SBMPN pathway, but are declared not to have passed, then you can re-register yourself to follow the independent path. Registration for the Polinema independent pathway is done online through the official address, namely http://admisi.polinema.ac.id.

  • MU/SMK/MA graduate and equivalent.
  • Participants are allowed to choose 4 (four) study programs.
  • Color photo file in school uniform size 4x6 (maximum file size 100Kb).
  • Not color blind for ENGINEERING registrants.
  • Participants from IPA are allowed to choose ENGINEERING and TRADE, IPS is only allowed to choose TATA TRADE and ENGINEERING (Specific Study Programs), Participants and LANGUAGE are invited to choose TRADE TATA, Vocational schools are allowed to choose a major/study program in the field of ENGINEERING or COMMERCIAL MANAGEMENT in accordance with their majors in SMK.
